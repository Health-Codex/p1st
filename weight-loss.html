How Our Program Works

A step-by-step approach to sustainable weight loss

1

Initial Consultation

Meet with our healthcare provider for a comprehensive evaluation including medical history, physical examination, and lab work as needed. We'll discuss your weight loss goals and any health concerns.

2

Personalized Plan Development

Based on your evaluation, we'll create a customized weight loss plan that includes dietary recommendations, exercise guidelines, and if appropriate, medication options.

3

Regular Follow-Up Visits

Scheduled check-ins to monitor your progress, address any challenges, and make adjustments to your plan as needed. We'll track your weight, measurements, and overall health improvements.

4

Ongoing Support

Continuous guidance and encouragement throughout your weight loss journey. We provide resources, answer questions, and help you overcome obstacles to achieve lasting results.

5

Maintenance Planning

Once you've reached your goals, we'll help you transition to a maintenance plan designed to help you sustain your results long-term with healthy habits.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our weight loss services

Is medical weight loss right for me?

Medical weight loss is appropriate for most adults who want to lose weight safely and effectively under professional guidance. It's particularly beneficial for individuals with weight-related health conditions like diabetes, high blood pressure, or high cholesterol. During your initial consultation, our healthcare providers will determine if our program is right for you based on your medical history and health goals.

How much weight can I expect to lose?

Weight loss results vary depending on individual factors such as starting weight, medical conditions, adherence to the program, and metabolism. Most patients following our program can expect to lose 1-2 pounds per week, which is considered a healthy and sustainable rate. Our focus is on helping you achieve lasting results rather than rapid weight loss that's difficult to maintain.

Will I need to take weight loss medications?

Not necessarily. Medication is just one tool that may be used in our weight loss programs, and it's not appropriate for everyone. Our providers will determine if medication might be beneficial based on your medical history, weight loss goals, and other factors. Many patients achieve significant results through dietary changes, exercise, and behavioral modifications alone.

How much does the program cost?

The cost varies depending on the specific components of your personalized program, including the frequency of visits, any necessary lab work, and whether medications are prescribed. Some aspects of medical weight loss may be covered by insurance, particularly if you have weight-related health conditions. We'll discuss all costs during your initial consultation so you can make an informed decision.

How long does the program last?

The duration of our weight loss program varies based on your individual goals and progress. Typically, the active weight loss phase lasts 3-6 months, followed by a maintenance phase to help you sustain your results. We work with you to create a timeline that aligns with your specific needs and weight loss targets.
