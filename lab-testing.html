Our on-site laboratory provides quick and convenient testing for a wide range of conditions. Get your results faster and start your treatment sooner with our comprehensive lab services.

Available Laboratory Tests

At People First Urgent Care, we offer a comprehensive range of laboratory tests to diagnose and monitor various health conditions:

Blood Tests

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP)
  • Lipid Panel (Cholesterol)
  • Hemoglobin A1C (Diabetes)
  • Thyroid Function Tests
  • Coagulation Studies

Urinalysis

  • Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Testing
  • Pregnancy Testing
  • Drug Screening
  • Kidney Function Tests

Infectious Disease Testing

  • Strep Throat
  • Influenza (Flu)
  • COVID-19
  • Mononucleosis
  • Sexually Transmitted Infections (STIs)

Benefits of Our On-Site Lab

  • Fast Results: Many tests provide results during your visit
  • Convenience: No need to visit a separate facility
  • Accuracy: State-of-the-art equipment for reliable results
  • Efficiency: Quick diagnosis leads to faster treatment
  • Cost-Effective: Often more affordable than hospital labs

Need Laboratory Testing?

Whether you need routine blood work or testing for a specific condition, our on-site laboratory is ready to serve you. No appointment necessary for most tests.
