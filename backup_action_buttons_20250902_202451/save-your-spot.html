Primary Care Appointment

For routine check-ups, chronic disease management, and preventive care. Schedule your appointment in advance.

Telemedicine Appointment

What to Expect

Confirmation

After reserving your time via NextPatient, you'll receive a confirmation with your appointment details.

Check-In

Arrive 15 minutes before your scheduled time to complete any necessary paperwork.

What to Bring

Please bring your ID, insurance card, and a list of current medications.

Wait Times

While we strive to see patients at their scheduled time, wait times may vary based on patient volume and acuity.
