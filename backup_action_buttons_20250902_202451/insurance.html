Accepted Insurance Plans

At People First Urgent Care, we accept most major insurance plans to make healthcare accessible and affordable for our patients. Our team will work with you to maximize your insurance benefits and minimize your out-of-pocket expenses.

  • Aetna

  • UnitedHealthcare

  • Kaiser Permanente

Government Programs

  • Medicare
  • Medicaid
  • TRICARE
  • Veterans Affairs (VA)
  • Workers' Compensation

Other Options

  • Health Savings Accounts (HSA)
  • Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA)
  • Self-Pay Options
  • Payment Plans

Please Note

This list is not exhaustive, and insurance coverage can change. We recommend contacting our office or your insurance provider to verify coverage before your visit. Please bring your insurance card and a valid photo ID to your appointment.

Self-Pay Options

Don't have insurance? No problem. We offer affordable self-pay rates for our services. Our transparent pricing helps you understand the cost of your care upfront, with no surprises. Please contact our office for current pricing information on all services.

Payment Policies

Co-Payments

Co-payments are due at the time of service. We accept cash, credit cards, and debit cards.

Insurance Billing

We will submit claims to your insurance company on your behalf. You are responsible for any charges not covered by your insurance.

Payment Plans

We offer flexible payment plans for patients who need assistance with their medical expenses. Please speak with our billing department to learn more.

Financial Assistance

We are committed to providing care to all patients, regardless of their ability to pay. Financial assistance may be available for those who qualify.
