Who We Are

Our Story

People First Urgent Care was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable healthcare services with a focus on patient comfort and convenience.

Our team of experienced healthcare professionals is dedicated to delivering personalized care that puts your needs first. We believe that healthcare should be accessible to everyone, which is why we've created a welcoming environment where patients of all ages can receive the care they need, when they need it.

From the moment you walk through our doors, you'll notice the difference that compassionate, community-minded care makes. We utilize the latest medical technology, streamlined digital tools, and an experienced clinical team to keep you feeling confident and informed every step of your visit.
