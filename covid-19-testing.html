Testing Options Available

We help you select the right test based on symptoms, timing, and requirements.

Rapid Antigen Tests

Ideal for quick screening with results during your visit. Recommended for symptomatic patients within the first few days of illness.

  • Results in as little as 15 minutes
  • Meets many employer and school requirements

PCR Molecular Tests

Laboratory testing with the highest level of sensitivity. Required for most travel, surgical, and procedural clearances.

  • Same-day collection with secure digital results
  • Results typically available within 24–48 hours

Antibody Testing

Blood testing to determine previous exposure or response to vaccination. Best performed 2–3 weeks after potential infection.

  • Quantitative results with provider interpretation
  • Helps guide future preventive care decisions

When Should You Get Tested?

Not sure if you need a test? Our providers can help you decide.

Experiencing Symptoms

Fever, cough, loss of taste or smell, congestion, or fatigue are common reasons to test.

Close Contact Exposure

Test 5 days after a known exposure, or sooner if you begin to feel ill.

Travel & Work Requirements

Meet documentation requirements for travel, return-to-work, or event participation.
