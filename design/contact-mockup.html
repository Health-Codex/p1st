Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to make an appointment?

No appointment is necessary for urgent care visits. However, you can save your spot online to reduce your wait time. For primary care appointments, we recommend scheduling in advance.

What insurance plans do you accept?

We accept most major insurance plans, including Medicare and TennCare. Please visit our Insurance page for a complete list of accepted plans.

What should I bring to my visit?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records or information about your condition.
