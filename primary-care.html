What is Primary Care?

Primary care is your first point of contact for healthcare. It's the ongoing care you receive from a healthcare provider who knows your medical history and can address a wide range of health concerns.

At People First Urgent Care, our primary care services focus on prevention, wellness, and managing chronic conditions to help you maintain optimal health. We build long-term relationships with our patients to provide personalized care that evolves with your changing health needs.
