Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my bill?

You should receive your bill within 30 days of your visit. If you have insurance, we will bill your insurance company first, and then bill you for any remaining balance after we receive their payment.

What if I have questions about my bill?

If you have questions about your bill or need assistance, please contact our billing department at 901-555-1234 during business hours (Monday-Friday, 8:00am - 5:00pm).

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans for patients who need assistance with their medical expenses. Please contact our billing department to discuss your options.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, all online payments are processed through a secure, encrypted connection. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, personal checks, money orders, and all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover).
