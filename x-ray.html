Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our X-ray and imaging services

Is an appointment required for X-ray services?

No appointment is necessary for X-ray services. You can walk in during our regular business hours or save your spot online to reduce your wait time.

How long does an X-ray procedure take?

The actual X-ray procedure typically takes only 5-10 minutes. Results are usually available within minutes, allowing for prompt diagnosis and treatment.

Are X-rays safe?

Yes, X-rays are safe when used appropriately. Our digital X-ray equipment uses the lowest possible radiation dose while still providing high-quality images. The benefits of accurate diagnosis far outweigh the minimal risks.

Does insurance cover X-ray services?

Most insurance plans cover X-ray services when medically necessary. We accept most major insurance plans and will verify your benefits before service. For patients without insurance, we offer affordable self-pay options.

Can I get a copy of my X-ray images?

Yes, you can request a copy of your X-ray images for your personal records or to share with other healthcare providers. We can provide digital copies or printed films upon request.
